Experience the Future

The gaming competition will take place at the Multipurpose Hall-2, AIUB, a spacious and well-equipped venue designed to host large-scale esports tournaments and gaming-related events. The venue will be transformed into a vibrant and high-energy environment to welcome players, fans, and sponsors.

Venue Setup and Experience

As attendees walk into the hall, they'll be greeted by the glow of LED lights, game-themed banners, and immersive audio effects setting the stage for a day full of digital action. The venue will be divided into several key sections:

Main Arena

A central stage with high-end PCs and consoles where headline matches and finals will be played live in front of an audience. Massive display screens will show real-time gameplay, commentary, and scores.

Player Zones

Designated seating for registered teams and solo players. Each zone will have professional gaming setups with ergonomic chairs, headsets, and cooling fans.

Spectator Area Rows of comfortable seating will be arranged for spectators to watch the matches, cheer for teams, and enjoy live commentary from shoutcasters.

Warm-up and Practice Zone

A separate space will be provided where competitors can practice before their matches. High-speed connectivity ensures no lag during crucial warm-ups.

Organizer Booth & Help Desk

Located near the entrance, this booth will assist with participant check-ins, schedule inquiries, and technical support.
